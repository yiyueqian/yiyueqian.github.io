Yiyue Qian

I am Yiyue Qian, a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Notre Dame, co-advised by Prof. Yanfang Ye and Prof. Chuxu Zhang. In 2017, I got my M.S. degree from University College London, London, UK. After graduation from UCL, I joined Ping An Technology, Shenzhen, China, as a NLP algorithm engineer from January 2018 to August 2019. In September 2019, I began my Ph.D. program in the USA. In the summer of 2022, I am doing my internship at Amazon, Seattle, USA, as a data scientist that focuses on developing graph representation learning models to detect fraud activities (e.g., seller-buyer collusion) and visualize the collusion patterns among bad actors.
